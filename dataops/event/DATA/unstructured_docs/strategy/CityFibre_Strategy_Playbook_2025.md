# CityFibre Strategy Playbook (Demo, 2025)

## Vision
Wholesale-only, neutral host full fibre platform enabling >8M UK premises, with resilient backhaul for homes, business, public sector, mobile, and smart city use cases.

## 2025 Priorities (Mid-year Update + site)

- Drive take-up: +102k net adds in H1 2025 to 620k total; push partner-led demand.
- Strengthen partner mix: deepen Vodafone, Sky, TalkTalk, Zen, toob, Cuckoo; onboard new ISPs.
- Backhaul & mobility: support small cell/Open RAN/fronthaul; resilient metro/long-haul dark fibre.
- Public sector & smart city: campus fibre, CCTV/IoT backbones, transport hubs, WiFi backhaul.
- Reliability & CX: SLA-backed DIA/Ethernet, proactive monitoring, faster installs.

## Market Position
- Differentiators: wholesale-only neutrality, high-speed tiers (up to 5.5 Gbps), dense metro rings, competitive backhaul pricing, partner choice.
- Competitors: BT Openreach/Virgin Media O2 (incumbent), Hyperoptic/Gigaclear (alt-nets), niche metro backhaul providers.

## Go-To-Market Motions
- Homes: partner-led FTTP offers with speed upgrades and reliability messaging.
- Business: DIA/Ethernet, resilient pairs, cloud on-ramps (AWS/Azure/GCP), SLAs.
- Public Sector: campus fibre, smart city backbones, PSN/PSTN migration to fibre.
- Mobile: 5G/4G backhaul, small cell/Open RAN fronthaul, edge DC access.

## KPIs (examples for the agent)
- Premises RFS; RFS velocity vs plan.
- Take-up: net adds, penetration %, churn.
- Partner performance: gross adds by ISP, ARPA/ARPU, install lead times, NPS.
- Network: uptime, latency/jitter, congestion, capacity headroom.
- Financial: build capex, revenue by segment/product, unit economics.
