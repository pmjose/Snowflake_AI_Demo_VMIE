# CityFibre Marketing Messaging (Demo)

## Core Value Props
- Full fibre speeds up to 5.5 Gbps; up to 95× faster than FTTC (website claim).
- Wholesale-only neutrality: choice of 30+ ISPs (illustrative partners: Vodafone, Sky, TalkTalk, Zen, toob, Cuckoo).
- Reliability: fibre end-to-end, fewer dropouts, proactive monitoring.
- Ready for the future: smart city backbones, mobile/5G backhaul, cloud on-ramps.

## Priority Campaign Themes (2025)
- Full Fibre City Launches: local awareness, speed/reliability, partner offers.
- Partner Acquisition & Co-Marketing: joint offers with ISPs, MDF-backed.
- Smart City & Public Sector: CCTV/IoT backhaul, campus fibre, transport hubs.
- Mobile/5G Backhaul: low-latency fibre for MNOs and Open RAN trials.
- PSTN Switch-Off Migration: fibre alternatives for legacy voice footprints.
- Cloud Connectivity: DIA/Ethernet plus cloud on-ramps for AWS/Azure/GCP.

## Proof Points (from provided reports/site)
- 4.6M premises RFS; target 8M.
- 620k consumer customers connected; +102k net adds in H1 2025.
- £2.3bn financing secured July 2025 to accelerate build.

## Tone & Guidelines
- Lead with reliability + speed; reinforce partner choice.
- Keep wholesale-neutral positioning; no retail offers.
- Use region-specific RFS stats where possible; avoid over-claiming.
- Cite official reports for numbers; note demo data where applicable.
