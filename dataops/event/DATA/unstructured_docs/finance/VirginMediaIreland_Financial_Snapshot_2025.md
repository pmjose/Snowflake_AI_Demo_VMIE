# Virgin Media Ireland Financial Snapshot (Demo)

- Virgin Media Ireland is Liberty Global’s Irish connected entertainment and communications business, providing broadband, TV, mobile and home phone nationwide.  
- Portfolio revenue is diversified across consumer broadband/TV bundles, SIM-only mobile, and business connectivity.  
- Full-fibre and DOCSIS upgrades are underway to deliver multi-gigabit (up to 5Gb) speeds with stronger reliability and lower churn risk.  
- Marketing investment is aligned to broadband + TV bundles and mobile cross-sell to defend share against eir, Sky, and altnets.  
- Capital is focused on fibre rollout, WiFi guarantee, and multiroom TV as differentiators.  
- Cash cost focus: network modernization, customer care efficiency, and content/licensing optimization.  
- All financials in euros (€); figures are illustrative and for demo only.
